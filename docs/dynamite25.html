In-depth Look at Twitter during AEW Dynamite #25




Here is the Twitter activity report for this week's Dynamite on Wed, March 25, 2020. The tweets were collected on Mar 25, 2020 from 05:59PM CST to 10:19PM CST, using the Twitter Streaming API with the query terms: #AEW, #AllEliteWrestling, #AEWDark, #AEWDynamite, #AEWonTNT, #WWE, #NXT. This produced a total of 49163 tweets, during the 4 hour and 19 minute timeframe.

This is part of an on-going project. The motivation behind this project is to capture user activity on Twitter during a major television broadcast and observe how well the program is resonating with their audience. The analysis can identify popular topics and hashtags, locate key users, and provide additional insights on how content is being shared. Feedback and comments are always welcomed.

Twitter Activity Drops From Last Week's Dynamite



This graph shows the number of tweets in the dataset. The vertical black dotted lines are reference lines for when the show started 07:00PM CST (or 19:00) and when it concluded 09:00PM CST (or 21:00). The graph shows a pretty consistent amount of activity throughout the show. There's a slight increases at 07:57PM CST (or 19:57) during the match between Brodie Lee (The Exalted One) and QT Marshall. The second increase in activity occurs between 08:51-9:01PM CST (or 20:51-21:01) during the exchange between Chris Jericho and Matt Hardy.

Top 20 #Hashtags





These tables show the 20 most frequently used #hashtags in the data set along with its frequency. Note that the hashtags underlined in bold were terms used in the query to collect tweets via the Twitter Streaming API.

The identified hashtags fall into three groups relating to AEW. First group shows the buzz is centered on the feud between The Inner Circle (#innercircle) and The Elite/Matt Hardy (#broken, #theelite, #brokenbrilliance), as shown by the popularity of their hashtags. The second group corresponds to the Judas Challenge (using hashtags #judaschallenge and #worldwidecrowd) which encouraged fans to sing-along to the song "Judas" during Chris Jericho's entrance. The third group has to do with Brodie Lee, also known as The Exalted One, (#joindarkorder, #exaltedone). He made his debut during last week's episode and this week featured his first AEW match.
